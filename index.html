GOT7 is a Kpop band produced under South Korean Entertainment company named "JYP". This group debuted in 2015. got7 have 7 members in total namely kim JAEBUM , Mark, Wang JACKSON, park JINYOUNG, YOUNGAE, BAMBAM, kim YUGEOM. And Kim Jaebum is Leader of this group. Got7 got Huge success around the world with their music & supportive from their Fans. Their fandom name is Aghasae(Bird). In 2021 they parted their ways from original company jyp.and this kpop band is still together as 7 and promised their fans to release more got7 music further as well. They will maintain both solo and group activities together.
